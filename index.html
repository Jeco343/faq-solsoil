FAQs

If you have a question, we might have the right answer for you.

  • How do I redeem points? Visit our rewards program page. Click the “Rewards” bar at the bottom of your screen to redeem (you’ll need at least 100 = $5).
  • How much soil do I need? Ask how many plants and how big they are. Use the rule: 1qt = 1x5" pot, 2qt = 2x5" pots, 1gal = 3x6" pots.
  • Do you ship internationally (outside the US)? We've paused international shipping while we sort out product registration. We hope to resume soon!
  • Do you wholesale? For wholesale pricing, visit solsoils.faire.com or email hello@solsoils.com.
  • Do your mixes need traditional soil? Nope! Our mixes are ready-to-use—no need to add anything.
  • Do I need fertilizer with your mixes? Yes! We recommend using our Sol Biotics line.
  • What's root rot? Root rot comes from overwatering or poor drainage.
  • How should I water your mixes? Water deeply but less often.
  • Can I get root rot with Sol Soils? Any mix can cause rot if overwatered.
  • What's in Sol Biotics? It's like a wellness kit for soil—nutrients, microbes, and even earthworm castings.
  • Do you offer promotions? We do! Check our Instagram for promo updates.
